Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 30, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to NEO Inc. Verification Bot ("we," "our," or "us"). We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot to connect your Roblox account with your Discord account.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our bot, we collect the following information:

  • Roblox Username/ID: This is collected through Roblox's OAuth feature when you authorize the bot to access your Roblox account information.
  • Discord ID: This is collected when you interact with the bot on Discord.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Linking: To link your Roblox account with your Discord account as per your request.
  • User Verification: To verify your identity and ensure proper functioning of the bot.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store the Roblox Username/ID and Discord ID in a secure database. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: We may use third-party services to help manage our database and perform other functions related to the bot's operation. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only as necessary to provide their services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

6. Your Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access and Update: You can request access to or update your Roblox Username/ID and Discord ID by contacting us.
  • Account Removal: If you no longer wish to use our bot, you can remove the connection by revoking the bot's authorization through Discord and Roblox settings. We will delete your information upon request.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website or directly communicated through the bot. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
